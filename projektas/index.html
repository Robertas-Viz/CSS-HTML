About Us

Our daily aim is to keep our team and others around us safe. Making sure that our team always have the right PPE. And before the job starts always looking for potential hazards. Safety is very important for us!

Our aim is to keep a good relationship with our clients and make them happy and satisfied with our work and workflow. We build our partnership with trust and excellent work we care about client!

Our team is highly trained and experienced in Rainscreen, Cladding, SFS. We keep our team up to date and always looking where we could improve more. Our management team has 20 years experience in Industry.

Why TerraClad?

We are specialist installers of all forms of Rainscreen Cladding bringing together years of experience into the management team. Our teams are highly experienced and well organized who are regularly updating their knowledge and skills through CITB. When accepting a job, it becomes our entire teams' goal to complete the job to the highest standard possible. Whenever we get contracted for a job, we have one focus that is kept in mind from the start of the job, until the very end. We specialize in giving old buildings a new lease on life.
